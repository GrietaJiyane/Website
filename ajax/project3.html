Sage Pastel Business Partner Conference
by Sage Pastel
Project Description
The conference brings business partners from all corners of the world together. The main purpose is to create a full understanding of the product by having sessions to update the channel on the developments of products (specifically Sage Evolution), training, consulting, support and also to honour performing business partners at the awards evening.
