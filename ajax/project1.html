App of the year awards
Project Description
The annual MTN Business App of the Year Awards has grown dramatically in size and stature since the inaugural event in 2012. Today, it is the premier app development event in South Africa. App Your Event provided the first ever event app for the awards which was a major success with a 98% download rate and the scratch card feature was a hit with attendees who won spot prizes through the app.
