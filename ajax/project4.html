Project Description
AfricaCom is Africa’s most notable tech event that gathers together 10,000 of the smartest, most switched-on digital shakers from the entire digital ecosystem. Supported by more than 350 of the biggest-hitting speakers in the digital world in Africa and beyond, AfricaCom has been chosen by more than 375 of the world’s most innovative, forward-thinking telecom brands to showcase their solutions and services. We provided an app for SES which was used for networking, showcasing their products and services and gamification.
