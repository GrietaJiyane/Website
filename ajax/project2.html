Oracle Partner Day

Project Description
This key event in the Oracle calendar, is a “must attend” for partners to learn more about how to make their business more profitable, as well as hear from senior Oracle executives on the latest key business up-dates at this packed full-day event. App Your Event provided the Event App and included Live Streaming which saw people from around the globe stream the sessions live to their device which was a great success.
